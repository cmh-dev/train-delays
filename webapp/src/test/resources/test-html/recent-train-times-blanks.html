Explanation

The statistic '% Arrivals RT-5m late' is the percentage of the time that the service arrived no more than 5 minutes late in the time period covered by this search (Mon - Fri between Mon 16 Dec 2019 and Mon 13 Jan 2020).

By this measure the best performing services are the 06:29, 06:48 and 06:48 departures from Haslemere, which arrived no more than 5 minutes late 100% of the time.

When looking at the average arrival time remember it is just the average. Sometimes the service arrived earlier than this, sometimes later.

Data Source and Validity

The information shown on Recent Train Times is sourced from Network Rail via their Open Data Feeds, augmented with additional data sourced from the Rail Delivery Group via their Darwin system.

Recent Train Times is not part of Network Rail or the Rail Delivery Group and is not an official rail industry body. Nor is Recent Train Times a source of official rail performance information / statistics.
